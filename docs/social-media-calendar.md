> THREAD: How to use COT data in your trading
>
> 1/ COT = Commitment of Traders. Published weekly by the CFTC. It shows positioning of institutional traders on futures.
>
> 2/ Watch the Commercials. They hedge, so extreme positioning often signals a reversal is coming. Contrarian signal.
>
> 3/ Large Speculators follow trends. When they're all-in one direction and commercials disagree, pay attention.
>
> 4/ Don't use COT for day trading. It's a weekly report. Best for swing and position trading bias.
>
> 5/ Combine with price action. COT tells you WHO is positioned. The chart tells you WHEN to act.
